About

Here are some of our journeys and stories of Say Story.

  • 2018-2019

    Profile

    Say Story is a signature drink innovation product in Indonesia, which was established in 2018 in the city of Solo. Say Story is committed to making signature drink products throughout Indonesia. For the price per cup of the Say Story drink, it can be said that it is very affordable, the price offered starts from IDR 12,000.

  • 2019-2020

    Story

    Say Story succeeds in presenting products with attractive raw materials and of course with such delicious flavors. Likewise, Say Story's signature drink product itself is still very broad in its market segment. Currently Say Story itself has around 300 outlets in Indonesia and it is not impossible that the existence of this hit Say Story drink will continue to grow.

  • 2020

    The journey of Say Story

    In addition to the cheap or affordable price, Say Story also offers many menu variants from Chocolate, Redvelvet, Greentea, Coffee, to Squash. Even Say Story also has a combination menu such as choco taro, redvelvet choco, and many more. Say Story until now has more than 300 outlets in various parts of Indonesia.
